# Micro Invasion
[Short Description]

Survive the microscopic onslaught! Become a rogue germ in this intense biological roguelike. Mutate your DNA, harvest nutrients, and outmaneuver the body's immune system. Can you evolve fast enough to trigger a total infection, or will the White Blood Cells purge you first?

# [About The Game]

Welcome to the microscopic frontier. In Micro Invasion, you are a lone pathogen navigating a hostile, ever-changing biological world. Every second is a battle for survival against the body's relentless defenses.

Eat to evolve, kill to grow stronger, and customize your genetic path. Will you become a fast-moving toxic cloud, or a massive, armored powerhouse that spawns an army of clones? The choice is yours in this high-octane, retro-styled survival adventure.
🧬 Evolve to Overcome

Unlock powerful mutations as you level up. From acid-spitting projectiles to "Enzyme Drain" lifesteal and "Frenzy" speed boosts, your DNA is your ultimate weapon. Experiment with different builds to find the perfect path to domination.
🛡️ Survival of the Fittest

Navigate past lethal hazards like corrosive alcohol zones, soap bubbles, and constricting peristaltic walls. It’s not just the White Blood Cells you have to worry about—the environment itself is trying to kill you.
👾 Retro-Future Aesthetics

Experience the infection through a nostalgic lens. Micro Invasion features a deep CRT display system with wavy scanlines, glass curvature effects, and a custom procedural sound engine that delivers a satisfying 16-bit punch.

# [Key Features]

  Dynamic Roguelike Gameplay: Every run is different with procedurally scaled enemies and randomized mutation choices.
  Deep Mutation System: Build your build with dozens of upgrades including Cytotoxic Bursts, Nerve Stuns, and Cloning Protocols.
  Intense Combat: Face off against standard germs, elite heavy hitters, and screen-filling Bosses.
  Tactical Inventory: Collect and use items like Adrenaline, Micro-Grenades, and Nanobots to turn the tide.
  Hardcore Difficulty: Take on "Hardcore" or "Nightmare" modes for the ultimate test of your biological prowess.
  Immersive Audio: Procedural soundscapes and spatial stereo audio provide a visceral sense of the microscopic world.
